# Financing Deck Request

## Company Overview
We are building **CertaStrategy** — an AI-powered strategic intelligence platform for solo founders and small teams.

## What We Do
CertaStrategy replaces the need for a full strategy, finance, and operations team by providing an AI command system that can:
- Generate investor decks, partnership proposals, and project definitions
- Route tasks through specialized AI departments (Strategy, Capital, Narrative, Product, Research, Audit)
- Deliver structured, auditable outputs with built-in quality control

## Stage
Pre-seed / Seed stage. We have a working V1 prototype (local Python engine) and are preparing to raise our first round.

## Market
- Target: Solo founders, micro-teams (1-5 people), indie hackers, and early-stage ventures
- Market size: 30M+ solo founders globally, growing 15%+ annually
- Pain point: These founders cannot afford McKinsey, Goldman, or a full C-suite — but they need the same quality of strategic output

## Business Model
- SaaS subscription: $99/mo (Pro), $299/mo (Enterprise)
- API access for power users
- Custom workflow marketplace (future)

## Ask
We are raising $1.5M in a pre-seed round to:
1. Build the cloud platform (move from local to hosted)
2. Expand to 15+ workflow types
3. Hire 2 engineers and 1 GTM lead
4. Reach 500 paying customers in 12 months

## Traction
- Working prototype with 8-department architecture
- 3 pilot users providing feedback
- Founder has 10+ years in strategy consulting

## Key Differentiator
Unlike ChatGPT or generic AI tools, CertaStrategy is a structured execution system — not a chatbot. Every output goes through a multi-department pipeline with built-in audit and quality control.
